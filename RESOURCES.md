## 🌊 Staying Current (don't drown)

The field ships something "game-changing" weekly. Here's a **sustainable** system — skim, don't binge:

**📰 Read (15 min/week):**
- 🆓 **[The Batch (DeepLearning.AI)](https://www.deeplearning.ai/the-batch/)** — Andrew Ng's weekly, signal over hype.
- 🆓 **[Hugging Face Daily Papers](https://huggingface.co/papers)** — the papers the community actually cares about, with summaries.
- 🆓 **[Import AI (Jack Clark)](https://importai.substack.com/)** — thoughtful weekly on where the field is heading.

**▶️ Watch (when curious):**
- 🆓 **[Yannic Kilcher (YouTube)](https://www.youtube.com/c/YannicKilcher)** — paper breakdowns, deep but accessible.
- 🆓 **[Two Minute Papers (YouTube)](https://www.youtube.com/c/K%C3%A1rolyZsolnai)** — bite-sized "what a time to be alive" hype-with-substance.

**📄 Skim papers in 5 minutes:** read the **abstract → figures → conclusion**. If still interesting, read the intro. You rarely need the math. Drop links into your study-buddy LLM and ask "explain the key idea and why it matters."

**🧠 The rule:** You will *never* be caught up, and that's the job. Follow 3–4 sources, ignore the rest, and learn deeply only what your current project needs. **Depth on demand beats shallow everything.**
